## Design Context

### Users
Primary audiences are Whistler residents, municipal decision-makers, prospective buyers, local businesses, and media. This is a public-facing proposal site used to explain a development vision, build trust, support proposal and approval conversations, and engage the broader community. The interface needs to work for an older audience and public-sector readers who may be scanning for clarity, credibility, and evidence rather than novelty.

### Brand Personality
The brand should stay aligned with Beedie Developers while remaining specific to the Northlands project through its approved color system. The emotional goals are trust, optimism, and excitement. The personality for design decisions should be treated as credible, polished, and community-minded.

### Aesthetic Direction
The current light-theme direction, approved palette, and home page visual language are fixed and should not be changed. Future design work should preserve the established premium-civic tone on the main site while improving the two newer concepts in `frontend/app/design-demo`. That means refined public-facing marketing design with strong readability, clear hierarchy, and enough visual distinction to feel contemporary without becoming flashy, youthful, or overly experimental.

### Design Principles
1. Preserve approved brand foundations on the home page, including theme and core color language.
2. Design for trust first: clear information hierarchy, legible typography, stable layouts, and restrained motion.
3. Make proposal content easier to understand for residents, decision-makers, and media through structured storytelling and scannability.
4. Keep visual expression polished and optimistic, but avoid aesthetics that feel speculative, trend-driven, or startup-like.
5. Meet WCAG AA expectations and prioritize readability for older audiences, including contrast, type size, spacing, and reduced cognitive load.
6. Focus enhancement work on the two designs in `frontend/app/design-demo`, not a wholesale redesign of the existing home page.
